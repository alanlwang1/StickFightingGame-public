Instructions

Welcome to the eloquently named Stick Figure Fighting Game! This was technically made by Daniel, Ryan, and Alan. But let's be real, Alan did 90% of the work. As our the name suggests, our game consists of stick figures fighting. The game will be split up between Draw Phases and Combat Phases. During Draw Phases, you will be allowed to add vertical/horizontal lines onto the screen, upon which your stick figures will fight on during the Combat Phase. This is currently a 2 player game, you will be given the choice between a Ninja and a Normal Character. Based on the option you choose, you will be either playing a best of three or a best of five. Player that kills the other will be allowed to draw an additional line in an intermediate draw phase.

Draw Phase

Note: At this moment, you can only draw vertical or horizontal lines. If you try to draw a diagonal, a straight line will be made in the direction of the greatest length.

You will need to draw using your keyboard

Player1

  • Press R to place down the first point, place a second point if the first is already placed, or draw a line between two points if both are already placed
  • Press F to cancel your drawing
  • Press UP DOWN LEFT RIGHT to move around your drawing cursor
Player2
  • Press SHIFT to place down the first point, place a second point if the first is already placed, or draw a line between two points if both are already placed
  • Press WASD to move around your drawing cursor

Press K to skip the drawing Phase

Combat Phase
